# Reference: IUPAC organic nomenclature in Catalan

`iupac-nomenclatura-organica-ca.pdf` is the authoritative short reference used to
build Catalan molecule names for this poem.

## Citation

- **Title:** *Guia breu de la nomenclatura de química orgànica* (Brief Guide to the
  Nomenclature of Organic Chemistry), Catalan translation.
- **Publisher:** IUPAC (International Union of Pure and Applied Chemistry), with the
  Catalan version prepared under the Societat Catalana de Química (Institut
  d'Estudis Catalans).
- **Edition/date:** 7th version; source PDF generated 2021-09-17 (filename
  `Guia-breu_CAT_7es_2_20211112`, 2021-11-12).
- **Source URL:** <https://iupac.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/Guia-breu_CAT_7es_2_20211112.pdf>
- **Mirror:** <https://iupac.qmul.ac.uk/BriefGuide/Translations/OrganicCatalan.pdf>
- **Retrieved:** 2026-07-11
- **Format:** PDF 1.5, 4 pages, ~0.9 MB.

## Why organic only

Most of the long, sonorous names this poem plays with come from organic chemistry
(alkanes, alcohols, acids, substituent locants). The inorganic and polymer brief
guides were intentionally left out for this poem.
